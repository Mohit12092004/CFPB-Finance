# 📊 CFPB Consumer Complaints Performance & Resolution Analysis


📌 Project Description
This project analyzes real-world consumer complaints submitted against financial institutions in the United States using the CFPB Consumer Complaint Dataset.The primary objective is to evaluate complaint resolution efficiency, measure response performance, and identify operational and data quality gaps in customer handling processes.


🎯 Business Objectives

Measure the percentage of complaints receiving timely responses

Evaluate average processing delay across companies and regions

Identify companies with strong or weak resolution performance

Analyze geographic distribution of complaints

Detect data quality issues such as missing state information


🛠 Tech Stack

Power BI Desktop – Dashboard creation & interactive reporting

DAX Query (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI calculations & measures

SQL – Data cleaning & transformation

AI Automation – Used to create automated KPI Cards and API-based dynamic visuals

Excel / CSV Processing – Data validation


🚨 Key Problems Identified

Based on business analysis and dashboard insights:

1️⃣ Complaint Concentration Risk

Over 87% of complaints recorded in 2025

77.41% complaints remain unsegmented

Limits demographic-level performance evaluation

2️⃣ Company-Level Risk (Block Inc. & Early Warning Services LLC)

High concentration of “Other transaction problem” complaints

Early Warning Services shows January spike (~12K complaints)

Sharp decline observed in subsequent months

3️⃣ Debt Reporting Risk

23.80% of total complaints are debt-related

High concentration among Equifax & TransUnion

Geographic clustering observed in Texas (9,000+ complaints)

Suggests aggressive recovery workflows & reporting inconsistencies

4️⃣ Segment-Level Observations

Servicemembers: 7.13% of total complaints

Older Americans: Higher average processing delay (3.02 days)

Texas shows highest complaint density


📂 Data Source

Source: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)

Platform: Kaggle – AI-Powered Complaint Resolution Dataset

Volume: ~200,000+ complaint records

Nature: Structured + Text-based consumer complaint fields

Dataset acquired from Kaggle under public CFPB complaint repository.
